RECRUITMENT PLAN

Department Admissions Plan

Integrating the unique strengths of the Department of Business Administration at Vanung University (two core modules, paid internships, and state-of-the-art AI labs) with my technical background, deploying AI Agents to innovate admissions.

🏫 Core Advantages of VNU BA

Our promotional efforts will focus on highlighting these four core departmental highlights:

  • Two Specialization Modules: Precision training in "Public Relations & Administration" and "Leisure & Catering Micro-Entrepreneurship."
  • Practical Certifications: Formulate workshops to pass Class-B certifications in Store Services, International Reception, and Strategic Marketing/Presentation.
  • Quality Paid Internships: Partnering with top companies (YourChance Pharmacy, Sen-Jia Catering, Min Tong Pharmaceutical, Sun-Field Consulting) for off-campus internships.
  • Premium AI Lab Equipment: Harnessing our "AI Smart Business Innovation Lab" and "Enterprise Simulation Lab" for hands-on, experiential learning.
🏫

Targeted High School Class Promotions

Focus on vocational high schools in the Tao-Chu-Miao region, using "VNU BA x Paid Internships x Certification Guidance" as the main theme. Showcase student modules and paid work experiences to build face-to-face trust.

🎪

Campus Expos & Experiential Booths

Set up booths with interactive "AI Smart Business Simulations." Harness student testimonial videos and mini-competitions on-site to boost engagement rates.

🤝

High School Alliances & Co-created Curriculums

Establish curriculum alignments and elective programs with high school business departments, integrating practical cases (e.g., AI applications, e-commerce) to build stable long-term recruitment channels.

📞

Follow-ups & Parent/Teacher Networks

Conduct warm follow-ups with candidates, guiding them and their parents/teachers to join official LINE for real-time consultation. Hosts parent visits to the AI Lab to address doubts and boost registration.

🎒

"AI × Business" Day Camps

Organizes summer and winter camps for high schoolers, letting them gain hands-on experience in our AI Smart Business Innovation Lab, demonstrating the modern appeal of VNU BA.

🏆

Project Competitions & Academic Incentives

Host vocational high school business competitions with VNU BA faculty guidance, and promote our rich entry scholarships and tuition waivers to attract top talents.

🤖

LINE AI Agent Admissions Assistant

Deploys a 24/7 LINE AI Agent. Built on LLMs, it precisely answers candidates about channels, thresholds, course modules, internships, and scholarships. Guides interested candidates to leave contacts for departmental follow-up.

🎯

AI Data-Driven Precision Ads

Leverages AI tools to analyze social interests and behavior tags of local high schoolers, delivering targeted campaigns via Meta and Google Ads, boosting the visibility of VNU BA advantages.

📹

AI Video Scripting & Reels Matrix

Employs AI tools to generate engaging scripts for TikTok and IG Reels (e.g. "A Day in the Life of a VNU BA Student", "Unboxing the AI Lab"), creating relatable short-form content matching Z-generation trends.

📧

AI Agent Automated Precision Marketing

Deploy AI Agents to automatically scrape and analyze regional high school admission trends and social hotspots, producing tailored content and running automated email/EDM campaigns.

📈

Admissions CRM Dashboard

Builds an admissions CRM tracking dashboard, measuring conversion rates of visits, digital ads, LINE queries, and workshops to adjust budgets dynamically.

🎮

AI Agent Specialized Workshops

Organize hands-on workshops where high schoolers build their own mini AI Agents or digital marketing projects, establishing VNU BA's tech-forward brand.

PHASE 1

📋 Preparation & Infrastructure (Months 1–9)

System Deployment & Material Planning

  • Construct and train the LINE AI Agent admissions knowledge base (integrating brochures and program specialties).
  • Produce the first batches of promotional short videos and posters showcasing the AI Lab and paid internships.
  • Assess surrounding high school departments to construct strategic alliance visitation lists.
  • Launch strategic high school visits, offering micro-courses and joint curriculum proposals.
PHASE 2

🔗 Promotion & Engagement (Months 10–21)

High School Visits & Media Matrix Operations

  • Initiate classroom visits in high schools, promoting the core concept of "VNU BA x Paid Internships x Certification Guidance".
  • Roll out the official LINE admissions assistant and AI customer service to provide 24/7 real-time answers.
  • Host "AI × Business" winter/summer day camps, inviting alliance high school students to practice using AI software.
  • Launch the social media matrix (IG, TikTok, FB, Threads) for daily operations, publishing internship achievements and academic news.
PHASE 3

🚀 Acceleration & Conversion (Months 22–30)

Classroom Visits & Precision Ad Spending

  • Open online portfolio guides and interview simulation workshops for candidates applying through individual application and screening channels.
  • Establish the "Parent and Teacher official LINE network" to answer queries about internship planning, tuition waivers, and employment opportunities.
  • Target prospective students and parents with precision advertising on Meta and Google Ads, emphasizing "tuition-free" policies and "paid internships".
  • Leverage the AI Agent to analyze click-through data and initiate automated email/EDM precision follow-ups.
PHASE 4

📈 Evaluation & Iteration (Months 31–36)

Data Analytics & Strategic Tuning

  • Provide online advisory services for the register-and-distribute channel to track prospective leads.
  • Launch the freshman care project to help admitted students apply for tuition waivers, dorms, and pre-courses, boosting the enrollment rate.
  • Leverage the CRM dashboard to evaluate conversion rates across channels (school visits, LINE, ads, camps) to measure ROI.
  • Evaluate overall recruitment efficacy and budget allocation to adjust strategies for the next admissions cycle.
