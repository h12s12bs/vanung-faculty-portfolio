RECRUITMENT PLAN

Department Admissions Plan

Integrating the unique strengths of the Department of Business Administration at Vanung University (two core modules, paid internships, and state-of-the-art AI labs) with my technical background, deploying AI Agents to innovate admissions.

🏫 Core Advantages of VNU BA

Our promotional efforts will focus on highlighting these four core departmental highlights:

  • Two Specialization Modules: Precision training in "Public Relations & Administration" and "Leisure & Catering Micro-Entrepreneurship."
  • Practical Certifications: Formulate workshops to pass Class-B certifications in Store Services, International Reception, and Strategic Marketing/Presentation.
  • Quality Paid Internships: Partnering with top companies (YourChance Pharmacy, Sen-Jia Catering, Min Tong Pharmaceutical, Sun-Field Consulting) for off-campus internships.
  • Premium AI Lab Equipment: Harnessing our "AI Smart Business Innovation Lab" and "Enterprise Simulation Lab" for hands-on, experiential learning.
