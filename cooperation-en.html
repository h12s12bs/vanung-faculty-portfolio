COOPERATION & INTERNSHIP

Industry-Academia Cooperation Blueprints

Connecting corporate resources, promoting mutual industry-academia research grants, and developing student internship pipelines to realize the core values of technical and vocational education in the Department of Business Administration at Vanung University.

🤝 Joint Project & Paid Internship Proposals

Customized collaboration proposals designed for local enterprises to boost operational efficiency and provide students with valuable industry experience.

01

YourChance Pharmacy Collaboration Blueprint

Proposal Concept: Developing intelligent pharmaceutical warehousing and store scheduling optimization systems based on Multi-Criteria Decision-Making (MCDM). Helps YourChance Pharmacy assess logistics and transfer priorities across Taiwan stores to reduce inventory losses. Provides paid internship opportunities for Business Administration students at store locations, guiding them to obtain class-B store service credentials, with direct transition to management trainee positions upon graduation.

MCDM Inventory Paid Internship Store Service Class-B Management Trainee
02

Sun-Field Consulting Collaboration Blueprint

Proposal Concept: Introducing Business Process Management (BPM) practical cases, assisting Sun-Field Management Consulting in SME diagnostics and digital transformation decision analysis. Jointly proposes NSTC industry-academia projects, engaging students in real management consulting practices. Guides students to earn marketing and presentation planning credentials during internships to nurture high-level consulting and strategy professionals.

BPM Consulting SME Diagnostics Marketing Strategy Planner Presentation Strategy Planner
03

Sen-Jia Catering (Bright House) Collaboration Blueprint

Proposal Concept: Deploying intelligent commerce systems, helping Sen-Jia Catering upgrade ERP portals and analyze delivery dispatch route optimizations. Utilizes big data to analyze customer purchase characteristics, optimizing menus and marketing resource allocations. Interns learn smart catering operations and receive entrepreneurship mentorship, accumulating skills in catering and hospitality micro-franchise management.

Catering ERP Route Optimization Catering & Hospitality Micro-Franchise
04

Bright Health & Min Tong Pharmaceutical Collaboration Blueprint

Proposal Concept: Deepening academic evidence and clinical outreach for patient Shared Decision-Making (SDM) systems, applying MCDM to chronic disease risk profiling. For Min Tong Pharmaceutical, assists in formulating procurement and supply chain management SOPs, optimizing media marketing budget decisions, and running research grants to drive brand modernization for the historic pharmaceutical enterprise.

SDM Systems Procurement SOPs Supply Chain Decisions Brand Modernization
🎒 Tripartite Internship Integration Pipeline

📈 High School - University - Enterprise Seamless Integration

Creating a tripartite pipeline that benefits high schools (by recruiting students), the university (by training them), and local enterprises (by hiring them).

🏫 High Schools

Phase 1: High School Foundations

• Connect with local commercial high school departments (e.g. Commerce, Marketing, Data processing).
• Provide high-schoolers with micro-courses (e.g. AI Office Applications, Digital Commerce Simulations) and school-enterprise tours.

🎓 VNU BA Dept

Phase 2: University Dual-focus Training

• Align university curriculum with business needs, utilizing VNU's AI Smart Commerce Lab.
• Guide students to obtain essential technical credentials (Store Services, Marketing Planners, Presentation Planners).

🏢 Enterprises

Phase 3: Corporate Paid Internships & Placement

• Place students in paid internships with collaborative enterprises (YourChance, Sen-Jia, Sun-Field).
• Leverage internships as pre-employment periods. Qualified interns are hired directly as management trainees upon graduation.
