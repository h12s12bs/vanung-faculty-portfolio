FUTURE RESEARCH

Future Research Plans

Adopting a three-phase strategy of "Technical Foundation, Cross-Domain Validation, and Industrial Deployment" to deepen the applications of advanced AI technologies in catering management, FinTech, and healthcare decisions.

01

🍽️ Intelligent Catering ERP & AIaaS Dynamic Scheduling Systems

Research Core: Combining Multi-Criteria Decision-Making (MCDM) models and reinforcement learning algorithms to develop intelligent catering operational decision systems based on an AIaaS (AI-as-a-Service) architecture. Formulates "dynamic scheduling" algorithms for highly variable food service environments, solving traditional ERP bottlenecks like peak foot traffic and labor shortages to upgrade catering digital management.

AIaaS Architecture Catering ERP Dynamic Scheduling Reinforcement Learning
02

📈 Intelligent Quantitative Decisions & FinTech Advanced Applications

Research Core: Exploring how machine learning and decision methodologies (e.g., Deep Reinforcement Learning DRL, Generative Adversarial Networks GAN) can be systematically integrated and applied to smart finance. Addresses complex problems in "dynamic trading strategies, asset allocation, and risk hedging" to assist financial institutions in constructing adaptive decision support systems.

Algorithmic Trading Fuzzy MCDM Asset Allocation Robo-Advising
03

🏥 Caregiver Dispatching & Intelligent Healthcare Scheduling Decisions

Research Core: Addressing long-term care dispatching and healthcare resource allocation under an aging society using big data analytics and operations research. Constructs multi-criteria scheduling evaluation mechanisms considering caregiver credentials, transit distances, and patient needs, optimizing resources to reduce waiting times and improve efficiency.

Elderly Long-term Care Healthcare Dispatching Operations Optimization Heuristic Algorithms
📅 Strategic Research Timeline

🚀 Three-Year Implementation Plan

Phased framework from academic models to industrial application deployment.

Year 1

Algorithm Design & Model Validation (Short-term)

Focuses on constructing fuzzy operations scheduling models and dynamic reinforcement learning heuristics. Validate algorithms against empirical datasets, write system prototypes, and draft papers targeting high-impact SCI/SSCI journals.

Year 2

System Prototyping & Field Verification (Medium-term)

Implement prototype systems under C# .NET/Web MVC, deploying decision engines in collaborative corporate settings (e.g., smart catering, FinTech brokerages). Draft NSTC project grant proposals based on initial pilot results.

Year 3

Cross-domain Deployment & Scaling Impact (Long-term)

Extend scheduling optimization platforms into long-term care caregiver dispatch systems. Promote digital management systems to more SMEs to generate industry consulting grants, bridging academic science with real-world business value.
