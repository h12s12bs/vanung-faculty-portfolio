📚 Academic Journal Publications

Key research results published in top-tier SCI/SSCI business administration and systems engineering journals.

01
SCI Q1 (IF: 8.5)

A hybrid MCDM model for assessing DASH diet adoption in hypertensive patients

Chun-Wei Chiu (First & Corresponding Author), Chin-Te Chang.

Expert Systems with Applications, 2025, Vol. 235, 121105.

This study proposed a hybrid Multi-Criteria Decision-Making (MCDM) model integrating DEMATEL, ANP, and TOPSIS. It analyzes the critical behavioral and physiological factors determining DASH diet adoption among hypertensive patients, providing healthcare administrators and clinical staff with personalized wellness strategy designs.

Hybrid MCDM DASH Diet Fuzzy Theory Healthcare Decisions
02
SCI Q1 (IF: 7.9)

Optimizing supply chain scheduling under stochastic demand using fuzzy logic

Chin-Te Chang, Chun-Wei Chiu (Co-Author).

Computers & Industrial Engineering, 2024, Vol. 190, 110045.

Focuses on operations scheduling optimizations under stochastic demand. By introducing fuzzy mathematics and linear programming, this paper constructs a robust model to solve scheduling bottlenecks, verifying its efficacy in real manufacturing and logistics supply chains.

Supply Chain Scheduling Fuzzy Programming Operations Optimization
